# Polybius square

* Each character is represented by a pair of numbers
* For example: A->11, B->21, C->31, X->35, Y->45, Z->55
* I and J both encode to 42
* How would we decode 3411334125423132?

|       | **1** | **2** | **3** | **4** | **5** |
| ----- | ----- | ----- | ----- | ----- | ----- |
| **1** | A     | B     | C     | D     | E     |
| **2** | F     | G     | H     | I/J   | K     |
| **3** | L     | M     | N     | O     | P     |
| **4** | Q     | R     | S     | T     | U     |
| **5** | V     | W     | X     | Y     | Z     |
